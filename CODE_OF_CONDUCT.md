# Code of Conduct

`tuprwre` welcomes contributors and users from all backgrounds.

- Be respectful and courteous in all project spaces.
- Focus on ideas and code, not individuals.
- Avoid harassment, intimidation, discrimination, and abusive language.
- Keep discussions constructive and professional.
- Report conduct concerns to the project maintainers promptly.

Enforcement is at maintainer discretion and may include warning, moderation, or removal from collaboration channels.
